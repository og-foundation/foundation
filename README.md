# OG

**OG (O'zbek Gurur)** is an independent institute dedicated to strengthening the culture of trust.

We help people make informed decisions through transparency, responsibility, and public benefit.

---

## Mission

To become the world's most trusted independent institute of public trust.

---

## Vision

A society where trust is earned through daily responsibility, transparency, and service to people.

---

## Core Principle

> **Trust is not a right.**
>
> **Trust is a daily responsibility.**

---

## Philosophy

### BBXB

> *Bo'lsa bo'lsin, Xalqqa bo'lsin.*

If we create something,

let it benefit people.

---

## What OG is not

OG is not a review platform.

OG is not a marketplace.

OG is not a social network.

OG is not a startup.

---

## What OG is

OG is an independent institute that designs trust.

---

## Our Promise

We do not judge.

We do not condemn.

We do not justify.

We show.

We explain.

We remain independent.

---

## Every contribution begins with one question

> **Will this help people trust each other a little more?**

If the answer is **no**,

it does not belong in OG.

---

## Foundation Principle

**Code serves principles.**

**Principles serve people.**
