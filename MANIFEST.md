# MANIFEST

> **We design trust.**

---

## Why OG exists

Every society depends on trust.

Trust allows people to cooperate, create, build businesses, share knowledge, and shape the future together.

Yet trust is fragile.

It cannot be bought.

It cannot be demanded.

It cannot be inherited.

It must be earned.

Every day.

OG exists because we believe trust deserves its own independent institution.

Not controlled by governments.

Not controlled by corporations.

Not controlled by popularity.

Only by principles.

---

## Our belief

Trust is not a right.

Trust is a daily responsibility.

Every person.

Every organization.

Every project.

Every institution.

Earns trust through consistent actions over time.

OG exists to make that process visible, understandable, and transparent.

---

## Our purpose

OG is building an independent institute of public trust.

Our mission is to help people make better decisions by providing transparent, evidence-based information about organizations, projects, and public figures.

We do not create trust.

People create trust.

We design the systems that help trust become visible.

---

## Independence

The value of trust depends on independence.

For that reason, OG shall remain independent from political influence, commercial interests, and personal loyalty.

Every decision must protect public trust before private benefit.

---

## BBXB

The cultural foundation of OG is BBXB.

> **Bo'lsa bo'lsin. Xalqqa bo'lsin.**

If we create something,

let it benefit people.

BBXB is not a slogan.

It is a principle for making decisions.

Whenever we face uncertainty, we return to one question:

**Will this benefit people?**

If the answer is no,

the decision does not belong in OG.

---

## We do not judge

OG does not exist to shame people.

OG does not exist to punish organizations.

OG does not exist to declare absolute truth.

Our responsibility is different.

We collect evidence.

We explain context.

We make information understandable.

People remain free to make their own decisions.

---

## Transparency

Every conclusion should be explainable.

Every rating should be supported.

Every status should have evidence.

Every standard should be public.

Transparency creates accountability.

Accountability creates trust.

---

## Responsibility

Every contribution to OG carries responsibility.

Every line of code.

Every document.

Every standard.

Every review.

Every decision.

We build systems that people may rely on.

Therefore every contribution must strengthen trust rather than weaken it.

---

## Long-term thinking

OG is not built for headlines.

It is not built for trends.

It is not built for rapid growth.

Institutions are measured in decades.

Every decision we make today should remain defensible many years from now.

---

## Our question

Before every decision we ask:

> **Will this help people trust each other a little more?**

If the answer is yes,

we continue.

If the answer is no,

we rethink.

---

## Our promise

We design trust.

Patiently.

Openly.

Independently.

For everyone.
